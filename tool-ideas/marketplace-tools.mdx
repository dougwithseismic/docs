## Supply & Demand Management

### Dynamic Inventory Balancer
**What it does**: Automatically adjusts visibility, pricing, and incentives to balance supply and demand across categories.

**Typical pricing**: $5,000-15,000/month for marketplace optimization

**Why prospects love it**: Prevents inventory buildup in slow categories while avoiding stockouts in hot ones.

### Seller Onboarding Accelerator
**What it does**: Streamlines seller verification, product upload, and listing optimization with AI assistance.

**Typical pricing**: $3,000-10,000 for onboarding system development

**Why prospects love it**: Reduces time-to-first-sale from weeks to days, improving seller retention.

### Demand Forecasting Engine
**What it does**: Predicts demand spikes and encourages sellers to stock accordingly with data-driven insights.

**Typical pricing**: $5,000-12,000/month for forecasting services

**Why prospects love it**: Reduces lost sales from stockouts while minimizing seller inventory risk.

## Implementation Tips

- **Solve for liquidity first**: Focus on tools that increase transactions and reduce friction
- **Build network effects**: Create tools that make the marketplace more valuable as it grows
- **Balance both sides**: Always consider impact on both buyers and sellers
- **Trust is currency**: Invest heavily in trust and safety features
- **Mobile-optimized**: Most marketplace activity happens on mobile devices
