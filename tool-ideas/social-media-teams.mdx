## Content Creation & Planning

## Community Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Comment Moderation AI">
    **What it does**: Automatically moderate and respond to comments

    **How it works**:
    - Categorize comments (questions, complaints, praise)
    - Auto-respond to common questions
    - Flag urgent issues for human review
    - Hide spam and inappropriate content
    - Generate response suggestions

    **Time saved**: 15 hours per week per account
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="DM Automation System">
    **What it does**: Handle routine DM conversations

    **How it works**:
    - Auto-respond to FAQs
    - Route complex queries to right team member
    - Send welcome messages to new followers
    - Handle contest entries
    - Track response times

    **Time saved**: 10 hours per week
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Influencer Outreach Tool">
    **What it does**: Find and engage with relevant influencers

    **How it works**:
    - Identify influencers in client niches
    - Track engagement rates and authenticity
    - Generate personalized outreach
    - Manage collaborations
    - Track campaign ROI

    **Time saved**: 20 hours per campaign
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
