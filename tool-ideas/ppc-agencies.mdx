## Campaign Research & Planning

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Competitor Ad Spy Tool" icon="eye">
    Automatically track competitor Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and LinkedIn campaigns. Pull ad copy, creative, landing pages, and estimate spend. Update weekly with changes.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Keyword Expansion Tool" icon="expand">
    Input seed keywords, automatically generate thousands of variations using search console data, competitor domains, and AI suggestions. Group by intent and theme.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Budget Allocation Optimizer" icon="calculator">
    Based on historical performance data, automatically suggest optimal budget distribution across campaigns, ad groups, and channels for the upcoming period.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Landing Page Analyzer" icon="file-code">
    Scan landing pages for conversion optimization issues, load speed, mobile responsiveness, and copy/CTA alignment with ad messaging.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Campaign Management & Optimization

<Note>
**Real-world success:** [Vouchernaut](/case-studies/vouchernaut-programmatic-ppc) automated campaign generation across 100,000+ brands, reducing creation time from 5 minutes to 10 seconds and driving 60% of affiliate revenue, ultimately leading to a Groupon acquisition.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bulk Ad Copy Generator">
    **What it does**: Generate hundreds of ad variations from a brief

    **How it works**:
    - Input: Product details, target audience, key benefits
    - Process: LLM generates headlines, descriptions, and CTAs following platform best practices
    - Output: CSV ready for bulk upload to Google/Facebook

    **Time saved**: 10+ hours per campaign
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Automated Bid Management System">
    **What it does**: Adjust bids based on custom rules beyond platform automation

    **How it works**:
    - Connect via APIs to ad platforms
    - Apply complex bidding strategies (dayparting, weather, inventory, etc.)
    - Execute changes automatically or queue for approval

    **Time saved**: 2-3 hours daily per account
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Search Term Mining Tool">
    **What it does**: Continuously analyze search terms for new keywords and negatives

    **How it works**:
    - Pull search term reports across all accounts
    - Identify high-performing terms to add as keywords
    - Flag irrelevant terms for negative lists
    - Group suggestions by theme and priority

    **Time saved**: 5 hours weekly per account
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reporting & Analysis

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cross-Channel Dashboard">
    Combine data from Google Ads, Facebook, LinkedIn, Microsoft Ads into unified dashboards. Calculate true ROAS including view-through conversions, assisted conversions, and offline sales data.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Anomaly Detection System">
    Monitor all accounts for unusual changes in spend, conversions, or performance. Get instant alerts when something needs attention instead of discovering issues during weekly reviews.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Client Report Generator">
    Pull performance data, generate insights using LLMs, create branded PDFs with commentary explaining what happened and what's planned. Include screenshots of top performing ads.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Quality Assurance Tools

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pre-Launch Checklist Automation">
    Scan campaigns before launch to verify:
    - Conversion tracking is properly set up
    - Landing pages are working
    - Ad copy doesn't have typos or policy violations
    - Budgets and bids are within expected ranges
    - Targeting settings match strategy
  </Step>

  <Step title="URL Parameter Builder">
    Generate properly formatted UTM parameters for all campaigns. Ensure consistent naming conventions across team members. Validate that parameters match your analytics setup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Account Audit Tool">
    Run comprehensive audits checking:
    - Account structure best practices
    - Keyword match type distribution
    - Quality score opportunities
    - Wasted spend identification
    - Extension utilization
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Client Service Tools

</Card>

<Card title="Opportunity Identifier">
  Weekly scan of all accounts to identify growth opportunities: new keywords from competitors, untapped audiences, creative refresh needs, budget increase potential based on impression share.

## Internal Efficiency Tools

<Info>
These tools help your team work faster and more consistently across all accounts.
</Info>

### Account Setup Automation
- **Template deployer**: Launch new campaigns from proven templates
- **Bulk settings copier**: Replicate settings across campaigns/accounts
- **Feed processor**: Transform product feeds for shopping campaigns

### Knowledge Management
- **Script library**: Centralized repository of Google Ads scripts
- **Test result tracker**: Document all A/B tests with results
- **Best practice enforcer**: Scan accounts for compliance with agency standards

### Team Collaboration
- **Task automation**: Generate task lists from account scans
- **Change log tracker**: Document all changes with reasons
- **Training simulator**: Practice account management in sandboxed environment

## Advanced Automation Ideas

<Tip>
These require more development but can become competitive advantages.
</Tip>

**Creative Intelligence Platform**: Use AI to analyze which creative elements drive performance (colors, copy styles, imagery types) and generate new creative briefs based on winning patterns.

**Predictive Budget Planner**: Based on seasonality, competition, and historical data, predict optimal budgets for next 3-6 months with confidence intervals.

**Competitive Intelligence Aggregator**: Combine data from SEMrush, SpyFu, Facebook Ad Library, and other sources into unified competitive intelligence reports.

**Multi-Touch Attribution Modeler**: Build custom attribution models that account for all touchpoints, not just last-click, to better understand true campaign value.
