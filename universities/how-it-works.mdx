# How Our Universities Work

## Learning Path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your University">
    Select the program that aligns with your career goals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enroll in the Program">
    Sign up for immediate access to all course materials
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn at Your Pace">
    Progress through structured lessons and exercises
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Projects">
    Apply your knowledge to real-world projects
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Certified">
    Complete assessments to earn your certificate
