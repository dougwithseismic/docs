<Tabs>

    We audit your entire analytics infrastructure to ensure you're capturing the data needed for growth decisions:

    We examine your marketing technology stack and processes:

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Product & Retention">

    We analyze how users interact with your product:

    - **Onboarding Flow**: Time to first value
    - **Feature Adoption**: Which features drive retention
    - **Engagement Metrics**: Daily/weekly/monthly active users
    - **Churn Prevention**: Why users leave and how to stop it
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Automation">
    ### Scale Without Headcount

    We identify manual processes that should be automated:

    - **Process Mapping**: Document current workflows
    - **Integration Gaps**: Systems that should talk to each other
    - **Time Analysis**: Hours wasted on repetitive tasks
    - **ROI Calculation**: Cost savings from automation
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="AARRR Metrics">

    We evaluate your entire growth funnel:

    - **Activation**: First meaningful engagement
    - **Retention**: User cohort analysis

  </Tab>
